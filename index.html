Game Rules

How to Play:

  • Players take turns rolling six dice
  • After each roll, player must select at least one scoring die
  • Player can choose to roll again with remaining dice or bank their points
  • If no scoring dice are rolled, that's a "Farkle" - player loses all points for that turn
  • First player to reach 5,000 points wins

Scoring:

  • Single 1 = 100 points
  • Single 5 = 50 points
  • Three 1s = 1000 points
  • Three of a kind (except 1s) = value × 100
  • Four of a kind = 2× three of a kind score
  • Five of a kind = 3× three of a kind score
  • Six of a kind = 4× three of a kind score
  • Three pairs = 1500 points
  • Straight (1-2-3-4-5-6) = 3000 points
